About

Global Bans is a service that connects all your game servers to a central ban list and admin panel. This allows you to manage all your bans from one place.

Global Bans is a free service that is available to everyone.

Features

Central Ban List

Connect all your game servers to a central ban list.

Admin Panel

Manage all your bans from one place.

Freemium

Global Bans is a free service that is available to everyone. Premium features are available for a small fee.
